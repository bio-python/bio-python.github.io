What is valuable to biology and why do we care?

  • When I say valuable, I mean the true cause of an effect observed. For example, we know that males and females are fundamentally differently in their biology because of the Y chromosome in males.

    • Presence of Y chromosome, determines whether a zygote develops into a male or a female.
      • Now you could say that we can determine this using karyotyping.
      • I agree but we can not answer any further questions.
      • (i.e) Which gene or genes cause this effect of the development?

    • Now that is not possible to answer with karyotyping.

    • Thanks to data science, we now know that the presence of “SRY” gene in the Y chromosome is the true cause of the effect.
